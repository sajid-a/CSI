Projects

The CSI SIESGST Student Branch started a new unit under its wing which would provide able and interested students a chanceto work on projects with real life applications and institute level projects.
The main aim of this step is to give the students hands on experience of what they learn during lectures, to widen their scope and to give them a feel of working in an organization.
CSI members of SIES GST interested in working on such projects can mail their resumes to csi.siesgst@gmail.com
with "Resume" in the subject field of the e-mail.
