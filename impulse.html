Impulse

We, at the Graduate School of Technology, are proud to be associated with CSI's first and only technical magazine. Impulse was launched in the year 2011. Just a year old, this magazine has already reached over 200 members in the college. As the membership to the CSI society has significantly risen over the last year, 'Impulse' is bound to cater to an even larger student and faculty body this year and in the years to come.

The main categories under Impulse are as follows:
1. Next-Gen Computing
2. Application and Game Reviews
3. Latest Creations
4. Green Computing
5. Hot Research Topics
6. Tips and Tricks

We are proud to present the members of CSI at SIES GST with Impulse 2012.

Those interested in publishing their articles in Impulse can e-mail their articles at csi.siesgst@gmail.com
with "Article for Impulse" in the subject field of the e-mail.
